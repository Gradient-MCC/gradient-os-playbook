‚ö° Quick Win #1: R.O.C.K. + Paste

Structure your context. Then show, don't describe.

‚ùå Typical approach
Analyze our Q4 campaign performance and recommend where to increase budget. We ran paid social, search, and programmatic.

Missing: success metric, actual numbers, audience, constraints, format. You'll get generic advice.

‚úÖ R.O.C.K. + Paste
[R] Performance strategist ‚Üí CFO [O] Recommend Q1 budget allocation. Goal: maximize ROAS, maintain reach. [C] [paste actual Q4 data table] CFO wants efficiency. CEO wants presence. [K] 3 scenarios. Trade-offs + risks. One-pager.
R ‚Äî Role

O ‚Äî Objective
What decision does this enable?
C ‚Äî Context
Paste data, emails, briefs‚Äîdon't describe
K ‚Äî Keys
Format, length, must-includes
